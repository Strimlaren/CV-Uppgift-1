gow-helper.


Automated stat data collector for Gems of War

open_in_new

Created to keep track of a multitude of statistical data when farming explore 12 for medal tokens in Gems of War on desktop. Sports a full GUI that enables the user to manually set drops, mimic ocurrence and no-drop runs. The app will keep track of current session stats, save everything on the fly in case of unexpected termination, and will on demand reset session drop stats and store them to the session history log where users can view their previous adventures.

The GoW-Helper app also offers automated data collecting by using OCR screen-reading technology to detect when the user has finished a mythic boss battle and received rewards. It will monitor what is shown on the screen and recognize when a reward screen is showing before collecting its data and automatically filling out the rewards in the manual controls for the user to double-check before he/she submits them to the session stats. It can also be set to automatically submit the stats, making it run fully automated.

The application needs the game window to be of a particular size and be located on the users main monitor. This is also handled automatically and the user will be warned if it is not able to track the game window. A log which shows what the app is detecting in the game, what is currently happening as well as runs duration and total drop value is also made available.

efexiaEQ.


Keeps track of multiple accounts and total EQuity across terminals.

open_in_new

Installing the (MetaTrader 5 only) application onto any number of terminals running on the same system allows the app to communicate the current equity levels of each account.

User can, via the GUI, select up to 4 accounts to show their live fed equity in the same terminal, eliminating the need for constantly switching between terminals. User can also, in the same window, select which of the accounts equity should be summed to a total equity. This makes risk management a lot easier, when handling several accounts at the same time whos total equity is subject to maximum drawdown limits. It is also possible to set account-specific equity-based alarm levels as well as sum total equity alarm levels. For convenience, a close-everything button is also made available.

soda machine.


Text-based soda dispenser game

open_in_new

Fictitous, text-based soda dispenser that can dispense individual soda cans as well as randomized/customized soda crates.

Navigation is all done through menus and several of the games functionality and behaviours can be manipulated in the settings menu. Remove or add new soda types, edit existing product prices, change max allowed money in the machine, how fast the prompts disappear and more.

Made in Python, the focus of this project was to make the entire machine with modular menus that will grow or shrink depending on the amount of products available. All menus are dynamically created and navigation adapts accordingly.

efexia excel data.


Exports MT5 terminal/account data to excel

open_in_new

Connect the Efexia Excel Data app to your MetaTrader 5 desktop Terminal and have it deliver live data to one or more of your spreadsheets, or combine data from several different accounts for view or to further process to your desires and needs. No programming knowledge required! The app enables you to connect your spreadsheets to an output .csv file, which means the app will be compatible with any Excel-type software that supports the extension.

Logs your Equity development during the day, with the log frequency of your choice. Built in risk management functionality. The app can keep track of your equity and drawdown, and automatically terminate all open positions and orders, while also alerting you on the platform and on the phone. Create any type of dashboard, with your own designs, layouts and data. Only your spreadsheet software and imagination set the limits. Sharing your MetaTrader common folder on the network enables datacollection and supervision of account performance and risk management of the entire office. Keep track of all in-house accounts on a single spreadsheet.
